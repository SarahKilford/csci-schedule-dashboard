    Academic Timetable


    Please read the following information carefully before using the Academic Timetable:

    Timetable formats to watch for:

    In Person courses will be noted on the academic timetable with time and location of the course.

    Synchronous (live) courses offer the class online at a specific time/day every week. Courses that are delivered synchronously are scheduled in Atlantic Time. Check this timetable for notes offering additional options for accessing course content.

    Asynchronous (recorded) courses do not require you to be online at a regular, specific time/day each week. Carefully review any notes on the timetable, and the course syllabus when circulated by the course instructor, on or before the first day of class, to see if there are time-specific individual events (like exams, office hours, workshops, etc.)

    Some courses have both in-person and online components (e.g. asynchronous lecture, in-person lab/tutorial). The format is noted on the timetable for each component.

    Make up days are additional instances of a course delivery to account for holidays. These are scheduled at the end of the term and will be added to the course delivery information before the start of the fall term.

    Cross listed Courses: Courses are cross listed based upon course content that deals with more than one subject area in a substantive way, and they are held together.


    Navigating the Timetable:
    To navigate this tool, use the buttons below to select a term and location. The best way to ensure you are seeing all courses within a department is to select “ALL” under Location.

    Please continue to check the timetable for the most up to date information.

    Need help selecting your courses? Contact an academic advisor.
